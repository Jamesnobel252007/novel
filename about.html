Meet the Author

Hi, I’m Nobee Chan—a writer, designer, and creative thinker who crafts fantasy worlds that spark curiosity, explore fear, and stretch the imagination. My storytelling blends mystery, magic, and emotional depth, drawing readers into eerie realms where nothing is as it seems.

I love building stories that aren’t just entertaining but leave you with questions. Why is that name in the book? What secrets lie behind the mirror? My debut novel is filled with such riddles—ancient visions, cryptic realms, and magical creatures that challenge reality itself. At the heart of my writing is the theme of transformation: of people, memories, and truth. If you enjoy tension, riddles, and eerie wonder—you’re in the right place.

Alongside writing, I have a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and responsive web design. I’ve crafted engaging web experiences—including this very landing page—focusing on smooth scrolls, animations, and clean visual storytelling. I understand how design enhances narrative, especially when it comes to showcasing a world or a brand.

My interests go beyond fiction—I enjoy philosophy, creative exploration, and deep thinking. I believe in asking strange questions, embracing ambiguity, and writing things that mean more the second time you read them. This mindset deeply influences my characters and the themes in my novel.

I’m currently learning JavaScript and aiming to master cloud integration. Whether it’s building interactive websites, crafting unique AI chatbots, or creating immersive digital content—I enjoy projects where I can combine creativity and technology.
